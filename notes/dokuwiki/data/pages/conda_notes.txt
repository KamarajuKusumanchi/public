Sample commands:

conda create -n <myenv> python=3.9 pandas pandas-datareader
</code>

==== commands I came across ====
<code>
$ conda config --add pinned_packages defaults::conda
</code>
To revert
<code>
$ conda config --remove-key pinned_packages
</code>
