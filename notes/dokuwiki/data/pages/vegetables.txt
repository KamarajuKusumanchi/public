==== zucchini vs. courgette ====
  * Both refer to the same vegetable
  * Zucchini is used in American English speaking countries
  * Courgette is used in french and British English speaking countries

==== eggplant vs. aubergine ====
  * Both refer to the same vegetable
  * Aubergine is used in British English
  * Eggplant is used in American English
