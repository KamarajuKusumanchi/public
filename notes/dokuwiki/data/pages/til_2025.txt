==== 2025-01-01 ====
TIL about "gunnysack". It is a large, simple bag made of rough material and traditionally used for carrying grain or vegetables [(https://dictionary.cambridge.org/us/dictionary/english/gunnysack)]. It is also known as a gunny shoe, burlap sack, hessian sack or tow sack [(https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gunny_sack)].
