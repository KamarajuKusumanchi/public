To get details on the number of I-526 petitions received, approved, denied, pending etc.,

<code>
https://www.uscis.gov/tools/reports-studies/immigration-forms-data
-> Filter by  category = EB-5 Immigrant Investor Program
-> Search
</code>

In the results, look for something like
  * "All USCIS Application and Petition Form Types (Fiscal Year 2021, 1st Quarter, October 1 - December 31, 2020)" . Sample link https://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/document/reports/Quarterly_All_Forms_FY2021Q1.pdf
  * "All USCIS Application and Petition Form Types (Fiscal Year 2021, 2nd Quarter, Jan. 1-Mar 31, 2021)" . Sample link https://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/document/reports/Quarterly_All_Forms_FY2021Q2.pdf

  * "Immigrant Petition by Alien Entrepreneur (Form I-526) Quarterly Report (Fiscal Year 2019, 2nd Quarter, Jan. 1-Mar. 31, 2019)" . Sample link https://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/USCIS/Resources/Reports%20and%20Studies/Immigration%20Forms%20Data/Employment-based/I526_performancedata_fy2019_qtr2.pdf

You can also go to https://www.uscis.gov/tools/reports-studies/immigration-forms-data via

https://www.uscis.gov/sitemap -> Search for "Reports and Studies" -> click on "Immigration and Citizenship Data" link.
