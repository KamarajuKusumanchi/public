==== socialization ====
Socialization requires skills... you need to be more like Carbon than Argon.

Carbon will form bonds with anyone it finds... oxygen, hydrogen, nitrogen... you name it. It builds long chains that are life of the party.

Argon just stands in the corner and does nothing. It does not know how to bond with others and vice versa.

If you want to socialize, just don't be picky and vice versa.

==== gold ====

Staying in cash OTOH is like leaving the veggies on the counter. Inflation will make them rot.
