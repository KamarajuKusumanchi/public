==== Moylan arrow ====
tags | fuel filler, fuel fill door

  * There is an arrow next to the pump on the fuel gauge that shows which side of the car the gas tank is on. It is called Moylan arrow[(wsj>https://www.wsj.com/business/autos/ford-gas-arrow-inventor-jim-moylan-6b2ef066)], named after Jim Moylan, a designer for Ford Motor Company.
  * Moylan
    * He invented the arrow in 1986[(wikipedia>https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fuel_gauge#Moylan_arrow)].
    * He was a Ford interior trim designer[(autoweek>https://www.autoweek.com/news/a69889877/gas-guage-little-arrow-inventor-has-passed-away/)].
    * He passed away December 11, 2025 at age 80 [(autoweek)].
  * Moylan arrow
    * The arrow debuted on the 1989 Ford Escort and Mercury Tracer, and soon showed up on virtually every car in the world [(autoweek)].
