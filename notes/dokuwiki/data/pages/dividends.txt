  * Qualified dividends are taxed at the long-term capital gains rate, which is 0%, 15% or 20%, depending on investor's income level.
  * Nonqualified dividends or ordinary dividends are taxed at investor's ordinary income tax rate, which can range between 10% and 37%, depending on the income level.
