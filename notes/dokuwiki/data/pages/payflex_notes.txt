==== Well-being Reimbursement Account ====

To get the list of eligible expenses for your Well-being Reimbursement Account, go to

  * https://www.mypayflex.com/HelpCenter/HelpAndSupport/ResourceCenter -> "Well-being Reimbursement Account Eligible Expenses"

The Resoure Center (https://www.mypayflex.com/HelpCenter/HelpAndSupport/ResourceCenter) can also be accessed via
  * Login -> "Help & Support" -> "Resource Center"
