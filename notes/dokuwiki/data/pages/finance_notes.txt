==== accredited investors ====
If you pass one of series 7, 65, 82 exams and the license is in good standing, you can become an accredited investor without having to worry about net worth or income requirements.

Ref:- https://www.investor.gov/introduction-investing/general-resources/news-alerts/alerts-bulletins/investor-bulletins/updated-3 - Accredited Investors – Updated Investor Bulletin
