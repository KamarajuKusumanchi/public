  * https://x.com/davidmarcus/status/1862654506774810641 - David Marcus on how Libra was killed.
  * The timeline:
    * June 2019: Facebook announced the Libra project. David Marcus was the lead.
    * Jan 2022: Facebook shut it down and sold the technology to Silvergate bank.
    * Jan 2023: Silvergate wrote it off.
    * Mar 2023: Silvergate was shutdown.
