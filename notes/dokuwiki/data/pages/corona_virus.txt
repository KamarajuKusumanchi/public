==== What is COVID-19? ====
COVID-19 stands for "coronavirus disease 2019." It is an infection caused by a virus called SARS-CoV-2. The virus first appeared in late 2019 and has since spread throughout the world. People in many areas have been told to stay home as much as possible in order to slow the spread of the virus.

Ref:- https://www.uptodate.com/contents/diffuse-large-b-cell-lymphoma-in-adults-beyond-the-basics#H2893604919

==== What are the symptoms of covid-19? ====

Fever, chills, cough, breathing difficulties, fatigue, muscle aches/soreness, headache, sore throat, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, loss of taste/smell, runny nose/congestion.

==== How many doses of Moderna's vaccine will be made? ====
600 million in 2021.

Ref:- https://www.reuters.com/article/health-coronavirus-vaccines-moderna/update-1-moderna-raises-2021-vaccine-output-forecast-to-at-least-600-mln-doses-idUSL4N2JF30F dated 2021-01-04

==== How many doses of Pfizer and BioNTech’s vaccine will be made? ====
50 million in 2020 and 2 billion by the end of 2021.

Ref:-
  * From https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2021-01-11/pfizer-biontech-boost-covid-vaccine-target-to-2-billion-doses dated 2021-01-11 \\ Pfizer Inc. and BioNTech SE plan to produce 2 billion doses of their Covid-19 vaccine this year, boosting previously expected output by more than 50% in response to surging global demand.
  * From https://www.pfizer.com/news/press-release/press-release-detail/pfizer-and-biontech-celebrate-historic-first-authorization dated 2020-12-11 -> "Manufacturing and Delivery Capabilities" section \\ Based on current projections, Pfizer’s and BioNTech’s combined manufacturing network has the potential to supply globally up to 50 million vaccine doses in 2020 and up to 1.3 billion doses by the end of 2021.
