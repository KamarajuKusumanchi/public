A Waffle slab is a type of reinforced concrete flooring system. It is recommended for buildings that require minimal vibration. \\
[1] - https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Waffle_slab

For example, the floor of a cleanroom in a semiconductor fab is typically built as a deep concrete waffle slab two to four feet thick! \\
