==== What is COVID-19? ====
COVID-19 stands for "coronavirus disease 2019." It is an infection caused by a virus called SARS-CoV-2. The virus first appeared in late 2019 and has since spread throughout the world. People in many areas have been told to stay home as much as possible in order to slow the spread of the virus.

Ref:- https://www.uptodate.com/contents/diffuse-large-b-cell-lymphoma-in-adults-beyond-the-basics#H2893604919

==== What are the symptoms of covid-19? ====

Fever, chills, cough, breathing difficulties, fatigue, muscle aches/soreness, headache, sore throat, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, loss of taste/smell, runny nose/congestion.

==== How many doses of Pfizer and BioNTech’s vaccine will be made? ====
50 million in 2020 and 2 billion by the end of 2021.
