==== 13F ====
  * The SEC requires all institutional investors that manage more than $100 million to file the form 13F within 45 days of the end of each quarter. [1]
  * It lays out firms’ equity holdings as of the end of the most recent quarter, as well as the size and market value of their positions. [2]
