==== clogged sink ====
  * Pour a full pack of baking soda in the sink
  * Pour double the quantity of vinegar
  * Let it sit for 15 mins.
  * Insert "The Drain Weasel hair clog tool" and clear the path for water to go down. Use the same to remove any clog (ex:- hair).
  * Once all the vinegar goes down the drain, pour some boiling water into the sink.
