===== configuration =====
==== facebook app ====
<code>
Settings icon -> Settings > Account Settings > Videos and Photos ->
    -> Video Settings -> Select Upload HD
    -> Photo Settings -> Select Upload HD
</code>

==== Email ====
Do not make sounds
<code>
Settings icon -> Notifications -> Email+
    -> Sounds -> disable
</code>
