==== 2023-10-24 ====
  * Two professors Randy Hatton and Leslie Hendele spent 20 years to prove that phenylephrine, a popular decongestant used in all sorts of OTC medications is ineffective.
    * https://www.theatlantic.com/health/archive/2023/09/cold-medicine-decongestant-phenylephrine-ineffective/675303/ - Why Has a Useless Cold Medication Been Allowed on Shelves for Years?
      * This is a fascinating article.
      * Instead of phenylephrine, it is better to use Sudafed. It contains a different drug, pseudoephedrine, that actually is effective against nasal congestion.
      * Sudafed is available OTC. But you have to ask a Pharmacist about it and show an ID to purchase.
