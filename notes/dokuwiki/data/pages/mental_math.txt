==== NJ sales tax ====
tags | NJ tax

You can mentally calculate the approximate NJ state tax by multiplying the price by (2/30) or ~\$2 for every \$30.

The exact sales tax in NJ is 6.625% and (2/30) equals to 6.67%.

<WRAP group><WRAP column>
^ price (\$) ^ exact tax (\$) ^ price * (2/30) (\$) ^
|   10 |  0.66 |  0.67 |
|   30 |  1.99 |  2.00 |
|   50 |  3.31 |  3.33 |
|  100 |  6.63 |  6.67 |
</WRAP></WRAP>
