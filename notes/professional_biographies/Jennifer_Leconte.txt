====== Jennifer Leconte ======
Created Sunday 13 September 2020

Jennifer Leconte, MSN, APN-C is a Nationally Certified Advanced Nurse Practitioner. After obtaining her Master of Science in Nursing at Seton Hall University, Jennifer worked as a Board Certified Registered Nurse specializing in neurology and neurosurgery. She then graduated from Monmouth University as an Advanced Nurse Practitioner and worked in interventional pain management and neurology.

Jennifer treats general neurological disorders but specializes in the treatment of headaches and migraine. She believes in creating quality treatment plans that are unique to the individual needs of her patients. She is trained in the administration of therapeutic Botox for migraine treatment as well as other muscular disorders. 

Jennifer is affiliated with Saint Peters University Hospital and is a member of the American Academy of Neurology.
