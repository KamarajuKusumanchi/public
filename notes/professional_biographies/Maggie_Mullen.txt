====== Maggie Mullen ======
Created Sunday 13 September 2020

Maggie Mullen began her career at St. Francis Medical Center in 1995 where she spent 13 years working in various areas including Cardiothoracics, Intensive Care, Critical Care, and the Emergency Department.  She worked in the Emergency Department for Capital Health System for 10 years providing care to critical patients, working in the Specialty areas of Neurology Emergency Department, Crisis, and Trauma as well as general Emergency Department patients.

She received her Bachelors degree from Thomas Edison State College in Trenton, New Jersey and her Masters degree from Simmons College in Boston, Massachusetts.
