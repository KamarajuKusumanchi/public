====== Joshua Hersh ======
Created Sunday 13 September 2020

Dr. Joshua Hersh is a graduate of SUNY Downstate Medical Center. After medical school, he completed his Neurology Residency and fellowship in EMG/Neuromuscular Medicine at Albert Einstein College of Medicine/Montefiore Medical Center. After completion of his training, he remained at Montefiore and served as an Assistant Professor in Neurology.

He is board certified in both neurology and electrodiagnostic medicine(EMG) as well as Neuromuscular . He has an interest in various aspects of neurology, but has specialized training in neuromuscular disorders, such as peripheral neuropathy, motor neuron disease, myasthenia gravis and myopathy.
