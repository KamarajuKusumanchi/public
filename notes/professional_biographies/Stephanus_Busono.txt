====== Stephanus Busono ======
Created Sunday 13 September 2020

Dr. Busono received his MD from Albert Einstein College of Medicine, NY in 1993. After an internship at the prestigous Cedars-Sinai Medical Center Los Angeles in Internal Medicine, he completed his  residency in Neurology at University of California San Diego. He then finished additional training as a fellow in Neuromuscular Medicine and Electromyography at the world renowned Cleveland Clinic Foundation under the mentorship of the late Dr. Asa Wilbourn, a pioneer in muscle and nerve disorders.
